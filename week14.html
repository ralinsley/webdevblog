Week 14

I think my biggest change if teaching 347 would be to have more of a specific focus on a specific part of web development so that more detail could be learned on the topic. I often felt rushed this semester going over so many different areas. Another change I would make would be to have more guided classes with lectures, mixed with something similar to the labs we did this year. I think the labs were helpful but there were so many and I felt I was only just getting a handle on the new subject so I would struggle with completing the labs. I think having a completion grade for the labs, combined with a significantly lowered amount of them would be helpful.

I think the allover textbook method of walking through a project was helpful, but it was hard for me to put together the different chapters of the book. I think something more traditionally going over new topics alongside the "project walkthrough" nature of allover would be helpful to the students.

Thanks for an interesting semester, and happy holidays!
